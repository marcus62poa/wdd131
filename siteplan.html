TrailSeeker Adventures - Website Plan

This is a quick and dirty plan document.

1. Site Name & Rationale

TrailSeeker Adventures
TSA

Description: The name works because it strongly suggests **exploration** and **outdoor activity** ("TrailSeeker"). It's memorable and relevant to the target audience (hikers, campers). Potential domain: trailseeker.guide

2. Site Core Purpose

The main goal is to be the **most trusted digital guide for advanced outdoor enthusiasts** in the Mountain West. It will provide **curated, up-to-date trail maps, critical gear lists, and essential backcountry safety protocols**. The purpose is to ensure users have all information needed for safe, challenging, and successful nature exploration.
